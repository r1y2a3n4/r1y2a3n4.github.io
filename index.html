Legends

George Best

George Best is considered by many to be one of the most talented winger the world has ever seen, including Pele himself. He scored 179 goals in 470 games and was part of the United Trinity. Towards the end of his career, he struggled against personal demons and sadly past away in November 2005

My favorite players

Here are some of my favorite players

Ryan Giggs

Ryan Giggs is the reason why I am called Ryan; my father loved this Welsh hairy chest winger. He played in an incredible 963 games and has scored 168 goals. he has won 13 English League, 2 Champions League and 4 FA Cups.


Paul Scholes

Paul Scholes is the true ginger prince. He is an exceptional inspiration for the younger generation. His partnership with Roy Keane devastated many midfields in England. Paul Scholes was one of the few truly world class English footballer.


George Best

Pele considered him to the best player in the world. That is coming from a footballer revere by many to be the best footballer ever. I love George Best because of his skills and his beautiful goals. Sadly he fall was bigger than his rise.

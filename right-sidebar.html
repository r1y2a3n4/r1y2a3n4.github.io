Sir Alex Ferguson is one of the greatest managers of all time. He build an empire that won everthing for Manchester United. He has won 13 Premier League titles, 10 Charity Shield 5 FA cups, 4 league cups, 2 Champions League, 1 FIFA World Club, 1 European Cup Winner Cup, 1 Intercontinental Cup. He was famous for implementing an atacking mindset on Manchester United.

Fergie was born in Glasgow,Scotland. He played as a striker during his young adulthood and was at one point top scorer in the Scottish Premier League. His first taste of managing came out a relatively young age of 32 when he managed East Sterlingshire. After a year, St. Mirren offered him to be their manager. He accepted and turned the club from languishing in the lower half of the Scottish Second Divivsion to Champions of First Divsions in 4 years. St. Mirren earns the distinction of being the only club that fired Alrx Ferguson, for mistreating his secretary.

Alex joined Aberdeen in June of 1978. He was joining a team that was relatively well run. He was feared by the players as a fierce disciplinarian, which motivated them to do better. He won the Scottish Cup 5 times and the league 4 times. Incredibly, he beat Bayern Munich in the semifinals and Real Madrid in the finals to win the European Cup Winner Cup. He is known to be the only manager to win a 'Double' in both Scotland and England and also as the last manager to manage a team that is not part of the Old Firm(Rangers and Celtic) to win the Sottish Premier League.

He left Scotland for Manchester United in 1986. Manchester United was in a rellegation battle and things were not looking great for the team. It took him 6 years before he won the Premier League. After that he never looked back.
